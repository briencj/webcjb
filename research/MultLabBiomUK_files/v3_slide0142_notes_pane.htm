The feature here is that Plots are to be randomized to I.R, I.T and I.R.T. If we do this completely at random, that is without regard to Lines, then Lines will be hopelessly nonorthogonal to Rows and Columns.
Cannot treat all contrasts of Block.Plots as a homogeneous set.
So have to use a design that takes into account all factors from first randomizations and make sure that contrasts of Block.Plots associated with Lines are balanced.
