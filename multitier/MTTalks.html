Slides for talks

  • Brien, C.J., Newell, N.J., Garnett, T., Watts-Williams, S.J., and Berger, B. Smoothing and extraction of traits in the growth analysis of noninvasive phenotypic data. Presented in August 2020 at The Biometry Hub, The Waite Campus, University of Adelaide, Adelaide. The presentation is based on the paper published in Plant Methods.
  • Brien, C.J. and Rogers, S. Designing comparative experiments using R: I. Concepts in experimental design PowerPoint or pdf; II. Nonorthogonal experimental design PowerPoint or pdf; III. Multiphase and partially replicated (p-rep) designs PowerPoint or pdf. Presented as a one-day workshop at Biometrics by the Botanic Gardens, International Biometric Society, Australasian Region Conference, Adelaide, S.A., in December 2019. The R package dae is available from http://chris.brien.name/rpackages and the package od from http://mmade.org.
  • Brien, C.J. The R-package asremlPlus: I. Mixed model selection; II. Prediction production & presentation. Presented at the SAGI Symposium, Rottnest Island, W.A., in May 2019. The R package asremlPlus is available from http://chris.brien.name/rpackages.
  • Brien, C.J. Mimicking anova in reml mixed-modelling of comparative experiments using the R-package asremlPlus. Presented at The Biometry Hub, The Waite Campus, University of Adelaide, Adelaide in September 2018 and a shortened version at the Australasian Applied Statistics Conference held in Rotorua, New Zealand in December 2018. The R script on which the talk is based is in Ladybird.anal.pdf. The Ladybird.dat data.set is available with asremlPlus via data(Ladybird.dat). The R package asremlPlus is available from http://chris.brien.name/rpackages.
