Description of subject

This subject will cover how to design agricultural experiments using the standard range of designs. The mixed-model analysis of these experiments will be derived and the following aspects of their analysis will be discussed: fixed versus random effects; model checking; analysis of treatment differences; determining the number of replicates; performing the analysis and designing the experiment in Genstat. Advanced topics in the design and analysis of experiments such as nonorthogonal designs and multitiered experiments may be included.
